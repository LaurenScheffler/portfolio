Search Engine Optimization

Search engine optimization (SEO) is a marketing practice that businesses execute online to help them rank higher in internet searches in relation to their niche. Businesses can resort to paying for sponsored results or organically reaching these results by using particular verbiage on their website and online presence to help drive more traffic and potential revenue their way. Since many individuals access search engines in their daily lives, many businesses can and do profit off of ranking high with search terms.

I have roughly 2 years of experience creating content with the ultimate goal of ranking higher in online searches for entertainment in Orlando. As a large city in America, Orlando has so many unique experiences that wide audiences are constantly looking for. Plus, it helps that Orlando is the Theme Park Capital of the World.
